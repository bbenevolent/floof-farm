Floof Farm TL Indigo Kat's dam is the lovely [CH Night Heron Farm JB Katniss](https://floof.farm/2021/07/10/ch-night-heron-farm-jb-katniss/) who is part of a long line of lovely udders. Katniss took Best of Breed at the [Roseburg Dairy Goat Association Show](https://roseburggoats.org/) this year in the ring judged by Emily Thompson-Healy. Another judge exclaimed "she's EIGHT!" at another show. If you are looking for longevity this is your guy. 

Indigo's sire [Sans Gene BV Time Lord](https://floof.farm/2021/07/07/tie/) who is a buck that adds width, dairy strength and size. The Kat line can be a small one, so this pairing is likely to balance that.

S: [SANS GENE BV TIME LORD](https://floof.farm/2021/07/07/tie/)
D: [CH NIGHT HERON FARM JB KATNISS](https://floof.farm/2021/07/10/ch-night-heron-farm-jb-katniss/)
