R Serenity Savanna Dry Cider *M (pending) is a lovely goat and herd favorite. She scored a GEEE87 as a 4 year old third freshener.
In June, 2025 Savvy went Grand Champion, Best of Breed and 1st Udder under Pete Snyder and Reserve under Amy Akramoff. This was out of a highly competitive class of 100 does. Let's see what the rest of 2025 brings!

Sire: Old Mountain Farm Blue Steel
Dam: R Serenity NHF Sparkling Cider
