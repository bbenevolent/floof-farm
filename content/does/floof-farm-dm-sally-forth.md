Sally Forth or SF as we call her is a little bit different lines for us here. She still traces back to some favorites like the Kat's but also has some unique to us milk lines on her dam's side. I'm curious to see what this outcross does when we freshener her. 

Sire's Sire: Frank Farm B Dalton Gun Metal

Sire: [Floof Farm DG Damascus](https://floof.farm/2021/07/07/damascus/)

Dam's Sire: Creamery Creek J Dragonfire

Dam: [Wild in Wild D Lay Down Sally](https://floof.farm/2021/07/07/wild-in-wild-d-lay-down-sally/)
